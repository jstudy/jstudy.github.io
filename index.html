Welcome to Xiaoheng Jiang (姜晓恒)'s Homepage


2018/01-now, I am an associate professor at School of Information Engineering, Zhengzhou University (ZZU), Zhengzhou, China.
2017/07-2017.12, I worked as a Lecturer at School of Information Engineering, Zhengzhou University (ZZU), Zhengzhou, China.
2013/09-2017/06, I was a Ph.D student at School of Electrical and Information Engineering, Tianjin University (TJU), Tianjin, China, under the supervision of Prof. Yanwei Pang.
2010/09-2013/07, I was a M.S student at School of Electrical and Information Engineering, Tianjin University (TJU), Tianjin, China, under the supervision of Prof. Yanwei Pang.
2005/09-2010/07, I was a B.S student at School of Electrical and Information Engineering, Tianjin University (TJU), Tianjin, China, my thesis is supervised by Prof. Yanwei Pang.
